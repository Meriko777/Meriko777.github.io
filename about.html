About Me

Experience in Armenia

My modeling journey began in Armenia, where I collaborated with different photographers, makeup artists, and various companies, bringing creative visions to life. I immersed myself in artistic projects, each experience shaping my path. Later, I joined WOT Modeling Agency, one of the most prestigious agencies in Armenia. I also had the privilege of modeling for the unique bridal brand Moment of White and the Italy-based fashion house Roberto Piraloff. Additionally, I appeared in several advertising campaigns, including a feature for Skyball Armenia.

Experience in Spain

In Spain, I had the incredible opportunity to walk in Bilbao Fashion Week, showcasing designs that reflected creativity and innovation. Following this, I became a model for SKFK, a renowned sustainable fashion brand known for its ethical approach and contemporary designs.
